Weekly Groups

Small groups. Same people. Real connection. Every week.

Every group at The Human Equation is built around one simple idea: you grow faster when you grow together. Our groups are intentionally small (around 10 people), confidential, consistent, and grounded in clinical insight and identity development.

When a group fills, it splits — creating more spaces for new members and leadership opportunities for those ready to step into the Builder role.
