The Human Equation Groups

Where identity, community, and growth come together.

Healing doesn’t happen in isolation. Most people don’t need another book or strategy — they need a consistent place to show up, be witnessed, and grow alongside others navigating similar seasons of life.

Each group inside The Human Equation is designed to help you rebuild identity, strengthen relationships, regulate emotions, and feel supported as you move through parenting, co-parenting, adulthood, or transitions.

All groups are included with Standard and Premium Memberships.

Monday Groups — Parenting & Family Identity

Parenting Autistic Kids & Teens

A compassionate, practical space for parents navigating neurodivergence, emotional regulation, sensory needs, school systems, and the pressure of being “on” all the time.

Parenting Teens

A steady, grounded space for the hardest stage of parenting — when your child is changing faster than you can adjust. Learn communication strategies, boundaries, and identity tools.

Parents of Trans & Gender-Nonconforming Youth

For parents committed to supporting their child's identity, safety, emotional development, and family relationships with clarity and compassion.

Parents Grief Circle (Sacred Space)

For parents grieving estrangement, identity shifts, or the loss of a relationship with a child. A quiet, respectful space for healing.

Survivors of Suicide (Parents & Loved Ones)

A grounding, community-based support circle for navigating the aftermath of suicide loss and reconstructing meaning after unimaginable grief.

Wednesday Groups — Identity & Belonging

20s Men’s Group — Identity, Purpose, Relationships

A guided space for young men learning emotional intelligence, role identity, boundaries, dating, career direction, and masculine development without shame.

20s LGBTQ Group — Identity, Belonging, Emotional Strength

A supportive, queer-centered community for self-discovery, relationship dynamics, emotional regulation, and chosen family.

Saturday Groups — Adult Identity & Transformation

Modern Fatherhood

Fatherhood is an identity — not a task list. This group helps men rebuild integrity, emotional regulation, co-parenting skill, and connection with their children.

Modern Motherhood

For women carrying emotional, logistical, and invisible labor. A space to reclaim identity, reduce overwhelm, and build a self that doesn’t disappear inside parenting.
