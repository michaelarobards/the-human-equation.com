Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade anytime.

No. Memberships are month-to-month.

Are these therapy groups?

No. These are professional-growth and peer-support groups led by Michael, grounded in identity reconstruction and relational intelligence.

What if I miss a week?

No problem. Come when you can. Consistency builds growth, but community holds you even when life interrupts.
