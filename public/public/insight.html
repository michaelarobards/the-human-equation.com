The Insight Series

Groundbreaking tools to understand yourself and others — in relationships, parenting, work, and life.

What is the Insight Series?

Developed over 40 years of clinical practice and organizational consulting, the Insight Series is a suite of assessments and workshops that illuminate the hidden patterns driving our lives. Combining personality type and temperament theory with attachment science, narrative therapy, and leadership development, this series offers a comprehensive framework for identity and relationship repair.

Each module combines accessible assessments with guided reflection, group discussion, and actionable tools. The goal? To help you understand yourself, understand others, and build healthier families, teams, and communities.

Modules

Personality & Temperament

Explore how the famous “alphabet soup” of personality types and temperaments influences how you think, decide, lead, and love.

Attachment & Emotional Patterns

Understand your attachment style and learn how it shapes your partnerships, friendships, and parenting.

Identity Reconstruction

Use the Identity Pie and narrative reconstruction to rebuild yourself after divorce, betrayal, or career upheaval.

Values Clarification

Play the “What Matters?” game to rank your core values and align your life with what truly matters to you.

Leadership & Professional Growth

Bring Insight tools into your workplace to build more cohesive teams, improve collaboration, and create shared vision.

Ethical AI & Modern Mental Health

Learn how the CARE-PES framework guides culturally sensitive, bias-aware AI applications in clinical settings.
