Membership: A Commitment to Growth

When you join The Human Equation you aren’t buying access to content — you’re making a promise to yourself and to the people you will grow alongside. Choose the level of engagement that meets you where you are.

Find Your Level

Community — $49/mo

For those who are curious about this work and need a gentle entry. You’ll have access to our recorded library, community forum and one live session each month to get a feel for what it’s like to sit in circle with us. Perfect if you’re exploring or not ready to commit weekly yet.

  • Access to recorded content and resource library
  • Community forum for connection & discussion
  • One live session per month
  • Admission to free community events & game nights
Join Community

Standard — $129/mo

This is where the real transformation happens. By attending every week you build trust, accountability and identity momentum. Your spot is held in a small group and you learn the skills that therapy alone can’t teach: emotional regulation, conflict mastery, humility based leadership. You also gain full access to our forum and curriculum.

  • Your own weekly live group (10 people max) with a professional facilitator
  • All community features: forum, recordings & monthly events
  • Identity & relational growth curriculum and exercises
  • Rotating leadership opportunities as you become ready
Join Standard

Premium — $199/mo

For those who want extra support and accountability. You get everything in Standard plus a monthly 30‑minute one‑on‑one coaching call with Michael. We’ll personalize your goals, check in on your progress and troubleshoot obstacles so you can keep growing at your own pace.

  • Everything in Standard
  • Monthly 30‑min 1:1 coaching call with Michael
  • Personalized goal-setting & accountability plan
  • Priority access to new groups & workshops
Join Premium

Have questions about which option is right for you? Get in touch and we’ll help you decide.
