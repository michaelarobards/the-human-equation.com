Our Story

The Human Equation stands on two generations of psychological work. Michael’s mother, Dr. Martine J. RoBards, pioneered the Insight System — a framework used worldwide to understand temperament and human differences. Michael carried this legacy forward through 20 years in clinical practice, developing an identity reconstruction model used in family therapy, men’s work, parenting frameworks, and community systems.

This community is the bridge between theory and real life — a place where people grow week after week through shared insight, structure, and connection.
